Metal Roof Coating Solutions

Professional roof coating services to protect and extend the life of your metal roof

CROMSEAL METAL ROOF COATING PROCESS

Step 1: Clean the surface

Use a high-pressure 3,000 psi water jet to remove dirt, grime, and old coatings without damaging the roof.

Step 2: First Layer Coat

Step 3: Second Layer Coat

Apply Acrylic Base Coat

Step 4: Third Layer Coat

Apply Acrylic Top Coat

Benefits of Our Coating System

Protection Against Corrosion

Prevents rust and corrosion in humid or coastal environments by creating a protective barrier against moisture.

Extended Lifespan

Shields from weathering, UV damage, and thermal expansion, significantly prolonging your roof's lifespan.

Waterproofing

Seals seams, fasteners, and vulnerable areas, making the roof completely watertight and preventing leaks.

Lower Maintenance Costs

Reduces repair frequency by addressing common issues like rust, leaks, and wear.

Improved Aesthetics

Enhances appearance with a clean, uniform finish available in various colors and styles.

Cost-Effective Solution

More affordable than full roof replacement, with minimal disruption to your operations.
