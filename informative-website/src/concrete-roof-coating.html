Concrete/Clay Roof Coating

WHY CROMSEAL CONCRETE ROOF COATING?

Extended Roof Lifespan: Protects tiles from UV rays, rain, and wind, reducing wear and tear.

Resistance to Mold, Mildew & Algae: Cromseal Fungikill helps inhibit fungal and algae growth.

Waterproofing & Leak Prevention: Seals cracks and prevents water penetration.

Enhanced Aesthetic Appeal: Revitalizes roof appearance with various finishes and colors.

Cost-Effective Maintenance: Reduces long-term expenses by providing durable protection.

Increased Property Value: A well-maintained roof adds to the curb appeal and value of a property.

CROMSEAL ROOF RESTORATION & COATING PROCESS

Step 1: Cleaning

Clean the entire roof surface with motorised "High-Pressure Water Jet" at 3,000psi gently stripping off loose particles, dirt, grime or previous coating without damaging the substance.

Step 2: Replacement

Replacement of broken/missing tiles at the affected area when necessary, realignment of misaligned tiles and repair and rectification work on ridge capping c/w sealing system where necessary.

Step 3: Coating

Spray "Cromseal Roof Coating System" on all roof tiles area only:

  1. 1st Coat: Cromseal Fungikill
  2. 2nd Coat: Cromseal Primer
  3. 3rd Coat: Cromseal Roof Coating (Base Coat)
  4. 4th Coat: Cromseal Roof Coating (Finish Coat)
